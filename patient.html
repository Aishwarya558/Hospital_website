Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I request medical records?

    You can request your medical records by filling out the Medical Records Request form available at the reception desk or on our website. Please allow 3-5 business days for processing.

2. Are there any support services available for patients?

    Yes, we offer a range of support services, including counseling, support groups, and patient education programs. Please speak to your healthcare provider for more information or refer to our Support Services page on our website.

3. What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

    In case of a medical emergency, please call 911 immediately or go to the nearest emergency room.
